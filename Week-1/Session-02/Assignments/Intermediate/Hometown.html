Seoni Malwa, Madhya Pradesh

Location

Seoni Malwa is a town located in the Hoshangabad district of the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. It is situated about 140 kilometers south of the state capital, Bhopal.

About

Weather

Seoni Malwa experiences a humid subtropical climate with hot summers and cool winters. The temperature during the summer months (March to June) can reach up to 45°C, while the winter months (November to February) are comparatively cooler with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C. The monsoon season (July to September) brings moderate to heavy rainfall in the area.

Population

As per the 2011 Census of India, the population of Seoni Malwa was 22,007. The town has a mix of Hindus, Muslims, and Jains who reside peacefully in the town. Hindi is the main language spoken in Seoni Malwa.

Sports

When it comes to sports, cricket is the most popular game in the town. There are several local cricket clubs that organize matches and tournaments, and many youngsters actively participate in them. Kabaddi, volleyball, and football are also played in the town.

Things to do
